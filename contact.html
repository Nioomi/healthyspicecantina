Who we are

We are a hispanic vegetarian, vegan, and gluten free cantina who offer plant based food. We have created an enviroment where people come together to relax, connect to wifi, study, hangout and EAT. We have been blessed with the opportunity to spread love to this planet and people through our fresh market recipes. Each reciepe is cooked with the perfect amount of spice and seasoning that will leave you wanting more. Eating here at Healthy Spice Cantina is an easy way to eat heathy with delicious flavor combinations.
